   - A one-liner headline that captures the site's essence. Something like: "Visceral, honest reactions to music — and the music is right there to listen to." Use large, bold typography (`text-3xl sm:text-4xl font-bold text-gray-900 dark:text-gray-100`).
   - One short sentence of supporting text below it in secondary color (`text-lg text-gray-500 dark:text-gray-400`), something like: "No ratings. No algorithms. Just people writing about songs that moved them."
   - A subtle link to /about: "Learn more" or "What is this?" styled as a text link (`text-gray-400 dark:text-gray-500 hover:text-gray-900 dark:hover:text-gray-100 transition text-sm`)

**About.jsx — Create the about page:**

1. Create `client/src/pages/About.jsx` as a new functional component
2. Use the same layout container as other pages: `<main className="max-w-2xl mx-auto px-4 py-12">`
3. Structure the page in three sections with generous spacing (`space-y-12` or similar):

   **Section 1 — What is Involuntary Response?**
   - Heading: "What is Involuntary Response?" (or just the site name as a statement)
   - 2-3 paragraphs explaining the site's purpose in editorial voice (music blog tone, NOT corporate):
     - The core idea: a place for visceral, honest reactions to music. Not reviews. Not ratings. Someone heard a song and needed to write about it.
     - Every post has the music embedded right there — you read the reaction, you press play, you decide for yourself.
     - It's a feed of musical moments, not a recommendation engine.

   **Section 2 — Who's behind this?**
   - Brief, personal explanation. First person or casual third person, not "Our team of passionate individuals..."
   - Something like: "Built by [a person/people] who got tired of algorithmic playlists telling them what to feel about music."
   - Keep it short — 1-2 paragraphs max.

   **Section 3 — Join / Subscribe**
   - Two clear CTAs:
     - "Join as a reader" — `<Link to="/join">` styled as a prominent text link or simple button
     - "Subscribe via RSS" — `<a href="/api/rss" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">` (link to the RSS endpoint)
   - Keep the CTA styling text-first. Could be two lines with arrow indicators or simple underlined links. Not big SaaS buttons.

4. Typography: Use `text-2xl font-bold` for section headings, `text-lg leading-relaxed text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300` for body paragraphs.
5. Import `Link` from `react-router-dom`.
